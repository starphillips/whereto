Frequently Asked Questions


Our mission is to leave no questions unanswered, we want to ensure that everyone understands how they can get involved, but most importantly how we can also make our service better for you!

We hope to cover everything, for anything else please feel free to get in contact!

1. Can you purchase tickets on Where To?

Not yet, but we are working towards offering ticket purchases directly from our website very soon!

2. What cities do you show events for?

We focus on Maastricht - for now - as this is where we study and have been passionate about improving the nightlife for students. We hope to be expanding very soon!

3. Where will we be able to hear any updates on Where To?

We will provide any updates on our social media pages, you can find these below!

4. How can an event holder get in contact about featuring events on Where To?

Our email, contact form or socials are consistently reviewed and ready for any collaborations!
