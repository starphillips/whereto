About Us

Behind the idea.

Let me introduce myself

Hello guys, I’m Jonas 21 and currently in my third year of studying IB at Maastricht Uni.

Another thing about me, I don’t like unorganised things. That’s why me and my wonderful girlfriend decided to build this website to make it easier for everyone, but especially the students to find and locate cool events happening in Maastricht.


What can we do for you?

We are really happy to finally start that journey. We tried to make it as easy as possible for people searching WHERE-TO go and for event holders to get word out easier, ensuring a fun and busy night!

If there is anything that you guys would like us to add or make better we would love to hear, as we grow our platform we want it to be supported by the people who use it!

- Jonas & Star
